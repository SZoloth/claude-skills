## Introduction (5 minutes)

**Welcome and context:**
"Thanks for taking time to talk with us today. We're trying to understand [topic area] better. There are no right or wrong answers - we're interested in your actual experiences."

**Set expectations:**
- "We'll be asking about specific times you've done [activity]"
- "Feel free to think out loud"
- "If something doesn't make sense, let us know"
- "We're recording so we can review later - is that still okay?"

**Background:**
- Role and responsibilities
- How long in current role
- Relevant experience

---

## Main Questions (35 minutes)

### Opening: Recent Experience

**"Tell me about the last time you [core activity we're studying]."**

**Follow-ups:**
- "Walk me through what happened step by step"
- "What were you trying to accomplish?"
- "What made that [easy/difficult/frustrating]?"

### Dig Deeper: Context and Pain Points

**"You mentioned [something they said]. Tell me more about that."**

**Follow-ups:**
- "How often does that happen?"
- "What do you usually do when that happens?"
- "What's the impact when things go wrong there?"
- "Have you found any workarounds?"

### Explore Patterns

**"Think about the last few times you've done this. What's typically [easy/hard/different]?"**

**Follow-ups:**
- "What varies from one time to another?"
- "What stays consistent?"
- "What would make this better?"

### Comparative

**"How does this compare to [related activity/previous tool/different context]?"**

**Follow-ups:**
- "What works better/worse?"
- "What did you wish you could do?"
- "What surprised you?"

---

## Reflection (10 minutes)

### Broader Patterns

**"Stepping back, what works really well about [topic area]?"**
- Capture what users love - preserve this!

**"What do you wish was different?"**
- Not asking for features - asking for desires outcomes

**"If you could wave a magic wand and change one thing, what would it be?"**
- Uncovers priorities

---

## Wrap-Up (5 minutes)

**"Is there anything we didn't cover that you think is important for us to understand?"**

**"Would you be open to us following up if we have more questions?"**

**Thank you:**
"This has been really helpful. We'll [what happens next with their feedback]."
